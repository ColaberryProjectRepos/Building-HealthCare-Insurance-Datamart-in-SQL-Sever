How to Prepare the De-normalized Data? 4

When looking to build a Star Schema data model, it is good to De-normalize (join) the data to ensure all of the tables are connected. This also makes it easier to see the full picture if you filter that data by one (For example: customer / student / owner etc...)  By joining all of the tables together in the model, we can confidently say that all of our data is related and view it from a data integrity perspective.
